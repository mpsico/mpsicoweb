About me

I'm Marita, I support people through their most difficult moments

I have been practising psychology since 2004. Over the years I have developed a way of working that combines clinical experience, specialist training and a warm, human approach adapted to each person.

Therapy is not just talking: it's understanding what is happening to you and working together to change it. Every process is unique, so I tailor the intervention to what you really need.

Training & specialisations

  • Forensic Neuropsychology
  • Trauma & EMDR
  • Disaster & Emergency Psychology
  • Mediation & Conflict Resolution
  • Psychogerontology
  • Motivational Coaching & Personal Mentoring
